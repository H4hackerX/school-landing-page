About TPHS

Established in 1985, Tauheed Public High School (TPHS) has been a cornerstone of educational development in Karachi. Our enduring mission is to cultivate a supportive and stimulating environment that champions academic excellence, instills strong moral and Islamic values, and promotes holistic personal growth.

We are dedicated to an educational philosophy that transcends rote learning, focusing instead on nurturing critical thinking, fostering creativity, and integrating core Islamic principles. Our goal is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and character necessary to navigate and contribute positively to the modern world.
